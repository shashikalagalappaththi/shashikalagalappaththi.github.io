Project information

  • Project: Tree Survey
  • Category: Surveys
  • Location: Beddagana, Sri Lanka

  • Contribution: Site Inspection | Tree Survey (Counting Trees | Numbering | Labeling | Mapping)

Description

The tree survey conducted in Beddagana Wetland Park offers an in-depth exploration of the rich tapestry of trees within this thriving ecosystem. By meticulously documenting each tree's species, age, health, and precise location, this survey provides a holistic view of the diverse plant life that graces the park.
