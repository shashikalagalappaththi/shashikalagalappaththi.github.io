Project information

  • Project: Green Building Awareness Program - 2019
  • Category: Awareness Program
  • Location: Sethsiripaya II, Sri Lanka

  • Contribution: Participated as an Organizing Member of the Program at Sethsiripaya Stage II

Description

The Green Building Awareness Program of 2019 was a dynamic and informative initiative dedicated to promoting sustainable construction and eco-friendly practices. Through a series of workshops, seminars, and interactive presentations, the program aimed to raise awareness about the significance of green buildings in conserving resources and reducing environmental impact.
